- Читаємо варінт відповіді репорта від АІ 
  ![report](./attachments/IMAGE%202026-04-21%2018:19:40.jpg)
  структура 0 5 параграфів:
  1. Introduction. Where, When, What. Simple present tense. Simple sentences.
     The line graph illustrates the popularity of four types of international food in the UK between 1965 and 2025: Indian, Chinese, Italian and Mexican cuisine.
  2. Overview ...of the main trends. Use: Overall, In general, It can be observed that, The most noticeable change is... Use simple past tense for completed periods.
    Overall, all four cuisines became significantly more popular over the 60-year period. Italian food appears to be the most popular by 2025, while Mexican food shows the most dramatic long-term growth.
  3. Details and comparisons. Use past tense for completed periods, present perfect for recent trends. Use adverbs to describe the strength of changes (sharply, steadily, slightly). Use comparative language (compared to, in contrast to, while...). Там використані approximately, roughly, about для приблизних чисел. Сильне використання respectively для позначення відповідності порядку данних і опису. 
     In 1965, Chinese food was the most popular at approximately 10%, whereas Mexican food was the least popular at only about 1%. Indian and Italian cuisine were also relatively uncommon, at around 5% and 8% respectively. However, all cuisines experienced steady growth. Indian and Italian food increased particularly sharply between 1985 and 2005. By 2005, Italian cuisine had reached roughly 68%, slightly higher than Indian and Chinese food. Mexican food grew slowly until 1985, but then rose rapidly from 8% to 70% by 2025.
  4. Reasons. Use: due to, because of, as a result of, this can be attributed to... 
    This trend may be due to increased immigration, foreign travel and greater cultural diversity in the UK. Supermarkets now sell a wider range of international ingredients, and social media has made global food trends more accessible.
  5. Future predictions. Use: It is likely that..., We can expect..., It is possible that... In my opinion...
    In my opinion, this trend will continue in the future. International food is likely to become even more popular as younger generations are more adventurous and open to trying new cuisines. We may also see the rise of other global foods becoming mainstream in the UK.
