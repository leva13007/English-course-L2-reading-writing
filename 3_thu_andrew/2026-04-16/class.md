- Idioms
  - "to be on the same page" - to have the same understanding of a situation
  - "break a leg" - a way to wish someone good luck, especially before a performance
  - "hit the nail on the head" - to describe exactly what is causing a situation or problem
  - "let the cat out of the bag" - to reveal a secret or a surprise
  - "piece of cake" - something that is very easy to do
  - "cost an arm and a leg" - something that is very expensive
  - "under the weather" - feeling sick or unwell
  - "the ball is in your court" - it is your decision or responsibility to do something
  - "burn the midnight oil" - to work late into the night
