---

*Silicon Valley* is an American comedy series created by Mike Judge. It stars Thomas Middleditch, T.J. Miller, and Kumail Nanjiani. The series is set in the tech world of Silicon Valley, California, and follows the story of a group of young programmers trying to build a successful startup company.

The main character, Richard Hendricks, accidentally creates a revolutionary compression algorithm and decides to start his own company, called Pied Piper. The plot is both funny and realistic — it shows the challenges of startup culture, competition with large corporations, and the bizarre personalities of the tech world. The acting is excellent, especially Thomas Middleditch as the awkward but brilliant Richard. The music is good and fits the modern, fast-paced atmosphere of the show. The ending of the series is bittersweet but honest.

Overall, I think *Silicon Valley* is one of the best comedies I have ever watched. I would definitely recommend it to anyone who is interested in technology or simply enjoys intelligent humour.

> *"1+1"* — це французький драматично-комедійний фільм про незвичайну дружбу між багатим паралізованим аристократом Філіпом та молодим хлопцем із передмістя Дріссом, якого Філіп наймає своїм доглядальником. ---

*The Intouchables* (known in Ukraine as *1+1*) is a French drama and comedy film directed by Olivier Nakache and Éric Toledano. It was released in 2011 and stars François Cluzet and Omar Sy. The film is set in Paris, France, in the present day.

The story follows Philippe, a wealthy aristocrat who becomes paralysed after a paragliding accident, and Driss, a young man from a poor suburb who is hired as his caregiver. Their relationship begins awkwardly but grows into a deep and genuine friendship. The plot is both moving and funny, which makes the film truly unique. The acting is outstanding — Omar Sy brings incredible energy and warmth to his character. The music, composed by Ludovico Einaudi, is beautiful and perfectly matches every emotional scene. The ending is uplifting and leaves the viewer feeling truly inspired.

In my opinion, *The Intouchables* is one of the most touching films I have ever seen. I would strongly recommend it to everyone.
