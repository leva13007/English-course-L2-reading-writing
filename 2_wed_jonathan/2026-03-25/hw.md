## Task

> **Correspondence** (Guide time: 50 minutes)
>
> You recently ordered a product online, but when it arrived, there were several problems with the item, the delivery and the customer service you received.
> Write a **formal email** to complain to the Customer Service Manager describing the problems and explaining what action you would like to be taken.
> Write at least **200 words**.

### Planning Checklist

Before writing, plan the following:

- [ ] Introduction — reason for writing
- [ ] Problem 1 — the item (what was wrong?)
- [ ] Problem 2 — the delivery (what went wrong?)
- [ ] Problem 3 — customer service (how were you treated?)
- [ ] Conclusion — what action you expect / deadline

---

### Useful Language (from class)

**Opening:**
- I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with…
- I am writing to inform you of a serious issue regarding…

**Describing problems:**
- I was extremely disappointed to find that…
- Not only was … but also…
- What is particularly concerning is that…

**Contrast / Concession:**
- I had expected a higher standard; however, this was not the case.
- This is unacceptable given that…

**Contrast / Concession:**
- I would appreciate it if you could… / I would like to request…
- Unless this matter is resolved promptly, I will be forced to take further action.
- I look forward to receiving your response within the next seven days.
- I trust that you will treat this matter with the urgency it deserves.
