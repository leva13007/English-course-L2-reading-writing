## Notes

- [ ] Use Past Simple after “It’s time” and “I’d rather (you)” even for present/future.
- [ ] “Despite” is followed by a noun or gerund, not a clause.
- [ ] Use a semicolon before linking words like “therefore”, “thus”, “consequently” when joining two sentences.
- [ ] Inversion is common after negative adverbials for emphasis (Hardly had..., Little did...).
