### Grammar Check

Fix the errors in these sentences:

1. Hardly I had stepped outside when it started to pour with rain.
2. I’d rather you don't smoke in the house if you don't mind.
3. It’s high time the government takes action against rising inflation.
4. She suggested to go to the new Italian restaurant in the city center.
5. Provided that you will finish your report by Friday, you can take Monday off.
6. The CEO, that I met at the conference last year, is stepping down next month.
7. Little he did realize that his discovery would change the world forever.
8. I am used to wake up at 5:00 AM every morning for my rowing practice.
9. Despite she had a broken leg, she managed to climb the stairs to her apartment.
10. Such was the force of the wind so that several trees were uprooted during the night.
11. Neither the manager nor the employees was aware of the security breach.
12. If only I didn't spend all my savings on that expensive car last year.

---

#### Corrected Sentences with Explanations

1. ✔️ Hardly had I stepped outside when it started to pour with rain.  
_After "Hardly" (meaning "ледве"), use inversion: Hardly + had + subject + V3._  
Після "Hardly" у значенні "ледве" використовується інверсія: Hardly + had + підмет + V3.

2. ✔️ I’d rather you didn’t smoke in the house if you don’t mind.  
_After "I'd rather (you)", use Past Simple for present/future wishes._  
Після "I'd rather (you)" використовується Past Simple для побажань щодо теперішнього/майбутнього.

3. ✔️ It’s high time the government took action against rising inflation.  
_After "It's (high) time", use Past Simple even for present/future meaning._  
Після "It's (high) time" використовується Past Simple, навіть якщо йдеться про теперішнє/майбутнє.

4. ✔️ She suggested going to the new Italian restaurant in the city center.  
_After "suggest", use gerund (V-ing), not infinitive._  
Після "suggest" використовується герундій (V-ing), а не інфінітив.

5. ✔️ Provided that you finish your report by Friday, you can take Monday off.  
_In conditionals after "if", "unless", "provided (that)", use Present Simple for future meaning._  
В умовних реченнях після "if", "unless", "provided (that)" використовується Present Simple для майбутнього значення.

6. ✔️ The CEO, whom I met at the conference last year, is stepping down next month.  
_In non-defining relative clauses for people, use "who" or "whom", not "that"._  
У неозначальних (non-defining) відносних реченнях для людей використовується "who" або "whom", а не "that".

7. ✔️ Little did he realize that his discovery would change the world forever.  
_After "Little" at the start, use inversion: Little + did + subject + V1._  
Після "Little" на початку речення потрібна інверсія: Little + did + підмет + V1.

8. ✔️ I am used to waking up at 5:00 AM every morning for my rowing practice.  
_After "be used to", use gerund (V-ing)._  
Після "be used to" використовується герундій (V-ing).

9. ✔️ Despite having a broken leg, she managed to climb the stairs to her apartment.  
✔️ Despite her broken leg, she managed to climb the stairs to her apartment.  
_After "despite", use a noun or gerund, not a clause._  
Після "despite" використовується іменник або герундій, не підрядне речення.

10. ✔️ Such was the force of the wind that several trees were uprooted during the night.  
_After "such ... that", do not use "so"._  
Після "such ... that" не використовується "so".

11. ✔️ Neither the manager nor the employees were aware of the security breach.  
_With "neither...nor", the verb agrees with the closest subject (here: employees — plural)._  
Якщо підмети з'єднані "neither...nor", дієслово узгоджується з найближчим підметом (employees — множина).

12. ✔️ If only I hadn’t spent all my savings on that expensive car last year.  
_For regrets about the past after "If only", use Past Perfect._  
Для жалю про минуле після "If only" використовується Past Perfect.

### Linking Words 3: Causal Conjunctions / Connectives

**Therefore, thus, consequently, as a result**

#### Examples

- Many households have started sorting their plastic and glass more diligently; **therefore**, the amount of waste ending up in local landfills has decreased significantly.
- Modern facilities can now process household waste into high-quality raw materials; **thus**, manufacturers can reduce their reliance on extracting new natural resources.
- Communities have invested heavily in solar-powered processing plants; **as a result**, the carbon footprint of the entire recycling cycle is lower than ever before.

#### Practice

Write your own sentence using a causal connector and a semicolon.

> Recycling is necessary to ensure that the products we throw away are reused in a sustainable way, thereby reducing the harmful effects on the environment caused by our waste.

---

### Semicolon ( ; ) — Usage

**What is the name of this punctuation mark?**  
; — semicolon

**When do we use it?**

1. **Joining Two Closely Related Independent Clauses**  
	- Example: The recycling bins were full; the truck was running behind schedule.

2. **With Causal Connectives**  
	- Use a semicolon before words like therefore, thus, however, consequently, and a comma after.
	- Example: The city improved its sorting facility; consequently, contamination rates dropped by 20%.

**The Rule:**  
Both sides of the semicolon must be able to stand alone as complete sentences.

**Strength:**  
- Stronger than a comma  
- Weaker than a full stop

### Writing Practice

**Ideas:**  
1. Living in a city ...  
2. Working from home ...  
3. Getting married young ...  
4. Retiring early ...  
5. Studying English online ...  

> Use all three linking types: addition, contrast, causal connection.  
> Use a semicolon.

**Example Paragraph:**

Studying English online offers a flexible schedule that allows students to learn at their own pace. **In addition**, digital platforms provide access to a global network of native speakers and diverse learning resources. **However**, staying motivated without the physical presence of a teacher can be a significant challenge for some learners. **Consequently**, students must develop strong self-discipline to ensure they complete their lessons consistently. Ultimately, mastering the language through virtual tools requires both a high-quality platform and a committed mindset.

### Reading Practice

**Two-paragraph text about Cardiff’s waste management**

Cardiff has undergone a significant transformation in its waste management strategy, moving away from the old "green bag" system toward a more rigorous "sack-sort" method. This new approach requires residents to separate their recyclables into three distinct containers: a blue caddy for glass, a red sack for metals and plastics, and a blue sack for paper and cardboard. In addition, the city has recently implemented stricter rules for businesses and workplaces, mandating the separation of food waste and small electrical items to meet the Welsh Government's ambitious 70% recycling target. However, the transition has not been without its hurdles, as many residents in high-density areas have expressed concerns about the lack of storage space for multiple bins and the tendency for lighter sacks to blow away during Cardiff’s frequent windy spells.

Despite these local challenges, the environmental data suggests that the shift is working, with contamination rates in some wards dropping from 30% to as low as 4%. Consequently, the council is continuing to expand these requirements, with new legal mandates for the separate collection of small waste electrical and electronic equipment (sWEEE) coming into full effect in April 2026. This push for "high-purity" recycling ensures that materials can be processed locally and kept within the circular economy rather than being sent to landfill. By combining these logistical changes with new services like soft plastic collections scheduled for later this year, Cardiff aims to solidify its position as one of the UK's leading cities for sustainable waste management.
