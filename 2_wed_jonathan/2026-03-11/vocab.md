# Vocabulary & Grammar — 2026-03-11 (Linking Words, City Life, Online Learning)

## Terms & Expressions

- [ ] associated with — connected to something (Living in a city is often associated with greater opportunities)
- [ ] a wide range of — many different types (urban areas provide access to a wide range of services)
- [ ] vibrant social environment — lively and active community
- [ ] drawbacks — disadvantages (Despite these drawbacks, ...)
- [ ] flexibility / flexible working arrangements — ability to adapt or change easily
- [ ] collaboration — working together
- [ ] productivity — efficiency, output
- [ ] effectiveness — how well something works
- [ ] motivation / discipline — reasons for acting / self-control
- [ ] convenient — easy to use, suitable
- [ ] limit (verb) — restrict (studying online can limit real-life communication)
- [ ] remains an effective option — continues to be a good choice
- [ ] renewable energy — energy from sources that are naturally replenished
- [ ] dependence on fossil fuels — reliance on oil, coal, gas
- [ ] coastal cities / low-lying islands — cities near the sea / islands not far above sea level
- [ ] evacuation — moving people away from danger
- [ ] reactor units — parts of a nuclear reactor
- [ ] power supply — source of electricity
- [ ] overheat / melt down — become too hot / fail dangerously
