# Homework Solution — 2026-03-11

## Task Summary

Write a five-sentence paragraph using at least one linking connector (e.g. moreover, in addition, furthermore) and one contrast connector (e.g. however, nevertheless, on the other hand).

---

## My Solutions

### 1. Living in a city

Living in a city is often associated with greater opportunities, although it may also present a number of challenges.  
**In addition**, urban areas provide access to a wide range of services, including public transport, healthcare, and entertainment.  
Many people are attracted to city life because of the numerous job opportunities and vibrant social environment.  
**However**, living in a city can be stressful due to high living costs, noise, and pollution.  
Despite these drawbacks, many people continue to choose city life because of the opportunities it provides.

---

### 2. Working from home

Working from home became common during the COVID-19 pandemic.  
**Furthermore**, it allowed people to save time and money on commuting and offered greater flexibility.  
Many employees felt more comfortable working in their own environment.  
**However**, some companies now require staff to return to the office, as remote work can reduce collaboration and productivity.  
Despite this shift, many people still prefer flexible working arrangements.

---

### 3. Studying English online

Studying English online has become increasingly popular in recent years, although its effectiveness often depends on the learner’s motivation and discipline.  
**Furthermore**, it allows students to access a wide range of resources and learn at their own pace.  
Many learners find online platforms more flexible and convenient than traditional classroom lessons.  
**However**, studying online can limit real-life communication and reduce opportunities for speaking practice.  
Despite these limitations, online learning remains an effective option for many students.
