Read each sentence and try to identify one mistake that needs correcting. 
1 If I would have known about the traffic, I wouldn't be late now. 
2 Not only he was late, but he also forgot his notebook. 
3 The painting is thought that it is a forgery by experts. 
4 By this time next month, I will have being working here for ten years. 
5 The thing what I found most difficult was the listening exam. 
6 He can’t have steal the car because he doesn't have a license. 
7 The number of rainfall this month is three times higher than average. 
8 Despite of the heavy rain, the match continued. 
9 I remember to lock the door before I left the house. 
10 You can choose however of these books you want. 
11 No sooner I had arrived than the meeting started. 
12 Give the prize to whomever finishes the race first.



---

1

❌
If I would have known about the traffic, I wouldn’t be late now.

✔
If I had known about the traffic, I wouldn’t be late now.

🧠
У third conditional після if не використовують would.

Формула:

If + past perfect → would have + V3

Але тут mixed conditional:

If I had known → I wouldn't be late now


---

2

❌
Not only he was late, but he also forgot his notebook.

✔
Not only was he late, but he also forgot his notebook.

🧠
Після Not only використовується інверсія (як у питаннях).

Not only + auxiliary + subject


---

3

❌
The painting is thought that it is a forgery by experts.

✔
The painting is thought to be a forgery by experts.

🧠
Після is thought використовують infinitive:

is thought to be
is believed to be
is said to be


---

4

❌
By this time next month, I will have being working here for ten years.

✔
By this time next month, I will have been working here for ten years.

🧠
Форма future perfect continuous:

will have been + verb-ing


---

5

❌
The thing what I found most difficult was the listening exam.

✔
The thing that I found most difficult was the listening exam.

або

✔
What I found most difficult was the listening exam.

🧠
what не використовується після thing.

---

6

❌
He can’t have steal the car because he doesn’t have a license.

✔
He can’t have stolen the car because he doesn’t have a license.

🧠
Після modal + have потрібна V3.

can’t have + past participle


---

7

❌
The number of rainfall this month is three times higher than average.

✔
The amount of rainfall this month is three times higher than average.

🧠
Rainfall — uncountable noun.

amount of rainfall

Не number.

---

8

❌
Despite of the heavy rain, the match continued.

✔
Despite the heavy rain, the match continued.

або

✔
In spite of the heavy rain, the match continued.

🧠

despite + noun
in spite of + noun


---

9

❌
I remember to lock the door before I left the house.

✔
I remember locking the door before I left the house.

🧠

remember doing → memory
remember to do → reminder


---

10

❌
You can choose however of these books you want.

✔
You can choose whichever of these books you want.

🧠

whichever = будь-який з
however = як / однак


---

11

❌
No sooner I had arrived than the meeting started.

✔
No sooner had I arrived than the meeting started.

🧠
Після No sooner використовується інверсія.

No sooner had + subject + V3


---

12

❌
Give the prize to whomever finishes the race first.

✔
Give the prize to whoever finishes the race first.

🧠

whoever — subject
whomever — object

Тут whoever виконує роль підмета.



===


Це завдання тренує linking words:
In addition / Furthermore / Moreover — всі вони означають “крім того / до того ж / більше того”.

Твоя задача — зʼєднати речення зліва (1–3) з правильним продовженням справа (A–C) так, щоб текст був логічний.

---

Спочатку зрозуміємо зміст

Речення 1

The tsunami that struck Japan on March 11th, 2011 knocked out the power supply at the Fukushima Daiichi nuclear plant.

Це початок події — цунамі вимкнуло електрику.

---

Речення 2

Following the disaster, the Japanese government ordered a large-scale evacuation of people living near the power plant.

Це наслідок — людей евакуювали.

---

Речення 3

Hydrogen gas built up inside several reactor buildings after the cooling systems failed.

Це технічна проблема в реакторах.

---

Тепер дивимося праву колонку

A

Furthermore, many towns in the area remained largely empty for years because residents were unable to return.

Це довгостроковий наслідок евакуації.

---

B

In addition, powerful explosions destroyed the roofs and walls of some of the reactor units.

Це продовження технічної проблеми.

---

C

Moreover, the failure of the cooling systems caused several nuclear reactors to overheat and melt down.

Це прямий наслідок втрати електрики.

---

Тепер поєднуємо

1 → C

Tsunami → power loss → cooling failure → reactor meltdown

✔

The tsunami that struck Japan on March 11th, 2011 knocked out the power supply at the Fukushima Daiichi nuclear plant.
Moreover, the failure of the cooling systems caused several nuclear reactors to overheat and melt down.

---

2 → A

Evacuation → towns empty for years

✔

Following the disaster, the Japanese government ordered a large-scale evacuation of people living near the power plant.
Furthermore, many towns in the area remained largely empty for years because residents were unable to return.

---

3 → B

Hydrogen gas → explosions

✔

Hydrogen gas built up inside several reactor buildings after the cooling systems failed.
In addition, powerful explosions destroyed the roofs and walls of some of the reactor units.

---

Правильні відповіді

1 → C
2 → A
3 → B
